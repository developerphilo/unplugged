Our Programs

We work towards eliminating the root cause of poverty and violence, advocate against all forms of inequality by acknowledging women’s values directed at realizing economical and social change facilitating access to accurate and timely information on sexual and reproduction health/rights

SEXUAL REPRODUCTION HEALTH & RIGHTS (SRHS)

Increasing awareness and knowledge about women’s and girl’s rights leading to a change in attitudes and practices. Our awareness raising activities find innovative ways to addressing the barriers in accessing information on SRHR, which result in lack of knowledge and skills to prevent unwanted pregnancy, HIV infection and GBV.

SEXUAL VIOLENCE:

Equipping survivors: Prevention of and response to sexual and gender based violence including addressing domestic violence, sexual harassment and Harmful Traditional Practices (HTPs) such as Female Genital Mutilations (FGM) and early and forced marriage.

Empower Individuals Support women and girl survivors of GBV as well as leading efforts to respond to GBV

Engaging boys/men Identify, recruit and train men and boys to serve as role models, positive influencers, change agents and mentors to address and prevent domestic violence, dating violence and sexual assault

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT:

Our POWER project Providing Opportunities for Women’s Economic Restoration - seeks to inform and raise awareness among women from the most vulnerable groups and help female entrepreneurs overcome the disadvantages they face in getting access to finance and support to grow their startups.
