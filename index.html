About Us

Share. Heal. Engage (SHE) We seek to use story telling as a forum for survivors of sexual violence and FGM to find their voice, find healing and enable them engage community stakeholders to join the fight against all forms of sexual violence

At UNPLUGGED, we create safe spaces that allow them to share their stories and find healing through a process that also empowers them to become grassroots advocates against sexual violence. By giving them space to speak out, we amplify their voices and ensure they confront their internal demons
